01 Dec 2025 (Monday)

Nasi Goreng Tomyam [AS Nasi Kandar] RM 7.00

(Dinner)

Their Wednesday rice isn't open. Patel suggested we come here.
Chose Tomyam as it was a safe bet, some prices got stickered with higher prices, such as Pattaya.

  • Lemongrass, Green chili, That inedible vege, but not found as much vs sky
  • Chicken, Vege, Onion, Carrot, Smashed tomato (a bit rare)
  • 1 Cucumber slice
It is quite spicy yes, but overall fine.
Their roti is open during night time, so that is also an option to try.

02 Dec 2025 (Tuesday)

Nasi Kandar [AS Nasi Kandar] RM 7.50

(Lunch)

From what I've heard yesterday they were planning to scram after lunch. Seems like my gut feeling was right.

  • Drumstick seems to have lower amount of meat
  • Regular rice, with goat curry
  • Just cabbage, the bois took 2 tofu, costed them an extra +1
  • Limau ais, still gotta think if its possible to take a non iced one or no
I can't tell if I'm slowly fading away from them, or I'm too self centered.
World doesn't revolve around me, but shouldn't I be in their orbits?

Nasi Goreng Ikan Masin [AS Nasi Kandar] RM 7.00

(Dinner)

The bois & another are having Tomorrow rice. I want to discover all the options they have.

  • 2 slices of cucumbers today
  • Chicken, and an amount of salty fish bones, there is some meat though (salty but nice)
  • Onion, Vege, Carrot, Fried onion, Red chili, Green chili
  • Weirdly a single smashed cherry tomato
The rice itself is ok, but the bones are... let's say "this is why we don't order this".
